About Me

Hi there! My name is Denis and I am an artist based in Nairobi, Kenya. With over 5 years of experience in the art industry, I have developed a unique style and passion for creating thought-provoking pieces that tell a story and engage the viewer. My medium of choice is acrylic , but I am also skilled in other mediums such as oil and clay sculpture and constantly exploring new techniques and materials to expand my artistic capabilities. In my career, I have had the opportunity to showcase my work in various galleries and exhibitions both locally and internationally, and I am always looking for new opportunities to collaborate and share my art with a wider audience. Outside of my art practice, I am also deeply committed to using my platform to give back to my community and am actively involved in various charitable initiatives that support the arts and promote accessibility and inclusivity.
